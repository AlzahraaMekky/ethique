About Egypte Ethique Tours

Established in 2004, Lady Egypt Tours is a boutique travel agency situated in the heart of Cairo. Lady Egypt is owned and managed by a husband and wife team of an Egyptian and Australian with more than 50 years of combined experience in serving tourists from all around the globe. Their knowledge and dedication were passed on and shared with the rest of the Nicolas family group of specialists that contribute to the continued success of Lady Egypt.

Meet Our Team

Our multilingual team of travel specialists are known to be very reliable, friendly and available from the very first moment of considering an Egypt holiday package. At Lady Egypt, we will share everything there is to know and guide you to finding the perfect holiday. We listen to your interests before offering Egypt travel packages that might fit your budget and interests, or handcraft and design your personalized itinerary while offering our advice along the way.

Our Egypt Travel Guides

At Lady Egypt Tours, we go to great lengths in choosing the right local guides as they spend the most time with our guests. They all still get rave reviews on many travel forums for their hospitality, professionalism, sense of humor, and especially their knowledge as they are all Egyptologists. All our guides are bilingual, they speak perfect English and Arabic as well as Spanish and French. They know each city by heart and are proud to share their heritage.
